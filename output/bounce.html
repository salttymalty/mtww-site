Bounce!

A 30-minute musical about a kid with endless energy

By two-time Richard Rodgers Award winner Dave Hudson

30 min
Runtime30 min

About the Show

Bounce! follows Reggie, a 10-year-old whose boundless energy drives everyone around them a little crazy — until the community discovers that what looks like a problem might actually be a superpower. A short, energetic show ideal for younger casts and shorter attention spans.
