Just So

Some of Rudyard Kipling's best stories brought to the stage!

About the Show

An adaptation of Rudyard Kipling's classic Just So Stories, exploring how various animals acquired their distinctive features — the kangaroo's legs, the cat's solitary nature, the rhino's itchy skin, and the elephant's trunk.
