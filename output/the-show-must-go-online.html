The Show Must Go Online

The world's first virtual children's musical — performed by over 1,000 productions worldwide

By two-time Richard Rodgers Award winner Dave Hudson

About the Show

A virtual children's musical written in 19 days during the COVID-19 pandemic. The Show Must Go Online became the most-licensed musical of 2020, performed by over 1,000 productions in 40 countries. Written for remote performance, it captured a moment in time when theater kids everywhere needed a way to keep performing.
