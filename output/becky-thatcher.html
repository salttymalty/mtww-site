Becky Thatcher

A new look at Mark Twain's Hannibal, MO — this time focusing on Becky instead of Tom!

About the Show

A stage adaptation of Mark Twain's classic, reimagined from Becky's perspective. Features familiar adventures like the whitewash scene while introducing audiences to the girls of Hannibal, Missouri. Tom Sawyer with none of the controversy.
