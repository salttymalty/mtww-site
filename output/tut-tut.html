Tut, Tut!

King Tut meets the Prince and the Pauper in this musical comedy

By two-time Richard Rodgers Award winner Dave Hudson

60 min
Runtime60 min
Songs13

About the Show

A musical comedy that imagines what happens when the young pharaoh Tutankhamun encounters a common boy who looks just like him. Inspired by Mark Twain's The Prince and the Pauper but set in ancient Egypt, Tut, Tut! is a fast-paced 60-minute show perfect for younger performers.

Why This Works for Young Performers

Curriculum-ready source material Based on The Prince and the Pauper / Ancient Egypt — connects naturally to classroom learning.

Curriculum Connections

Pair this show with classroom learning:

  • Social Studies — Ancient Egypt
  • Language Arts — Mark Twain adaptation
