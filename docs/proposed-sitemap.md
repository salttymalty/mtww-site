## Page Purposes (Redesigned)

### Home `/index.html`
**Job:** Get a teacher from "I'm looking for a show" to "this one looks right" in under 30 seconds.

Sections (in order):
1. **Hero** — "Find the right musical for your students" + immediate CTA
2. **Quick Browse** — Filter chips: by grade level, by subject area, by cast size
3. **Featured Shows** — 3-4 cards with comparison-ready metadata
4. **What's in a Production Kit** — Brief visual summary (the product)
5. **Why Educators Choose MTWW** — 4 cards reframed from teacher perspective
6. **Educator Resources** — 3 featured articles
7. **Testimonials** — Ready for real quotes (structural placeholder)
8. **Final CTA** — "Read any script free — no commitment"

### Shows `/shows.html`
**Job:** Let a teacher compare all available shows by the criteria that matter to them.

Sections:
1. **Filter bar** — Grade level, difficulty, themes/subject
2. **Show cards** — Richer cards exposing: age, cast, runtime, difficulty, themes, curriculum tags, price
3. **Other Works** — Dave's BBB catalog (clearly marked as external)

### Show Detail `/{show-id}.html`
**Job:** Give a teacher everything they need to decide "is this the right show for my program?"

Sections (reordered):
1. **Hero** — Title, tagline, credential line, quick-spec summary
2. **At-a-Glance** — Spec card (ages, cast, runtime, songs, difficulty, price)
3. **Synopsis** — What the show is about
4. **Why It Works** — Show-specific reasons this works for young performers
5. **Curriculum Connections** — Subject area ties + learning outcomes
6. **Production Kit** — What's included + pricing + order CTA
7. **Director's Notes** — Practical staging/casting info (from FAQ data)
8. **FAQ** — Remaining questions
9. **Free Perusal CTA** — "Read the script free before you commit"
10. **Related Shows** — Cross-links

### For Educators `/resources.html`
**Job:** Help teachers find guides relevant to their current need.

Grouped by purpose:
- **Choosing a Show** — Selection guides, comparison frameworks
- **Directing & Staging** — Show-specific director's guides, staging tips
- **Building a Program** — Philosophy, pedagogy, program-building advice

### Production Kits `/licensing.html`
**Job:** Definitive reference for what you get and how the process works.

Sections:
1. What's in every kit (checklist)
2. How it works (4-step process)
3. MTWW vs traditional publishers (comparison table — single location, not duplicated)
4. FAQ (licensing-specific only)

### About `/about.html`
**Job:** Build trust through story, credentials, and people.

Sections:
1. Origin story (AG → MTWW)
2. Awards & recognition
3. Founders
4. Collaborators
5. The AG connection

### Contact `/contact.html`
**Job:** Make it easy to reach out with zero friction.

Three paths:
1. Read a script free (primary CTA)
2. Order a Production Kit
3. Learn about The Actors Garden
